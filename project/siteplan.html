Site Purpose

ByteBites simplifies complex technology topics like AI, blockchain, and emerging innovations for beginners. The goal is to make cutting-edge tech accessible to non-experts, students, and professionals.

Scenarios

  • How does AI impact everyday life in 2025?
  • What is blockchain, and how does it work?

Color Schema

  • Deep Blue (#1E3A8A) - Header, footer, navigation
  • Bright Cyan (#00C8E0) - Links, accents, hover effects
  • Light Gray (#F5F5F5) - Background
  • Dark Gray (#333333) - Text color

Typography

  • Poppins (Sans-serif) - Used for headings
  • Roboto (Sans-serif) - Used for body text
  • Courier New (Monospace) - Used for code snippets
